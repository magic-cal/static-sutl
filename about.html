About the league

What is SUTL

The aim of the Southern Universities Trampoline League is to provide competitive trampolining to university students across the South of the UK in an ever-evolving format that is run by students, for students. The SUTL committee exists to provide a trampoline league that benefits all university students and supports and facilitates university clubs in hosting competitions.
